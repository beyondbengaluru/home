Beyond Bengaluru
Key facts, company pipeline and investment data for the BB vertical
Mission
Attract companies, investments, and talent to Karnataka's districts beyond Bengaluru through policy, infrastructure and ecosystem building.
Clusters
6 BB Clusters: Mysuru · Mangaluru · Hubli-Dharwad-Belagavi · Kalaburagi · Tumakuru · Shivamogga — each covering 4–6 districts.
Key Policies
Karnataka IT/ITeS Policy 2024 · Startup Policy 2022 · GCC Policy 2024 — providing fiscal, land and infrastructure incentives.
